Crystal Game

Instructions

You will be given a random numer at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal below you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your score to the random numer. You lose the game if your total score goes over the random number. The value of the crystal is hidden until you click it.
